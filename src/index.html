ITE THE VIOLINIST

ABOUT ME

Ìtéolúwakìíshí (popularly called “Ite the violinist” or “Ite”) is a multi-genre violinist, multi-instrumentalist, and musician. Ite was born in Lagos, Nigeria to music loving parents who exposed him to genres of popular music at an early age. To further hone his love for music, they rewarded him with his first musical instruments: a guitar and a saxophone, for emerging top of his class.

Ite specializes in performing for wedding ceremonies and enjoys tailoring his performances to the interests and style of each couple. Whether you desire a close-knit indoor ceremony or a stunning outdoor ceremony with sophisticated multi-genre music, Iteoluwakiishi can play delightful sounds to make your special day even more memorable. Ite is an amazing entertainer who knows how to share the beauty and energy of music with his audience. He stretches the boundaries of the violin with fusions of classical, pop, blues, and jazz in his music.

As a classically trained violinist who is certified in both theoretical and practical violin from the Associated Board of Royal Schools of Music (ABRSM), Ite has collaborated with several leading wedding planners in Nigeria, including 2706 Events, Oaken Events, MarcuSmith Events, June5ive Events, and TrendyBee Events.

His various musical talents have gotten him recruited to play alongside the String Quartets, Violin Concertos and as a soloist with chamber groups. He has also been sought after to lend his skills to several household names like Nathaniel Bassey, Sinach, Lawrence Oyor, Tony Elumelu, Dija, Emma OMG, Naomi Mac (The Voice season 3), Precious Emannuel (The Voice), Abiodun Koya, Kingdom (winner of Nigerian idol), Dapo Pelmar (Nigerian Idol) and Ibk sings.

With over a hundred thousand views on his YouTube channel and a reputation for providing musical entertainment for all occasions, Ite is one of the most sought-after violinists in Nigeria. Having performed at hundreds of events ranging from weddings to coronations, anniversaries, church concerts, and state house ceremonies, he has the needed expertise for every event.
